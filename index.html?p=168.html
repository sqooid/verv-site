Introduction

The eCommerce industry continues to grow at an increasing rate each year. Its growth is supported by a robust payments industry that facilitates digital payments for goods and services. Business owners need to look into how they can use a payment solution to facilitate online shopping.

A report published by Statista shows that e-global retail sales will hit a total global value of $4.8 trillion by the end of this year. These statistics show that this is the right time to invest in a competent digital payment system.

What Is a Digital Payment Solution?

Tech advancements have enabled the world to become more digital, leading to more choices when conducting a financial transaction. A digital payment solution refers to a system that facilitates online payments between payer and payee.

The use of an online payment eliminates the need to exchange physical cash between two or multiple parties. Additionally, it makes it easier to transfer digital currency using mobile technologies from one account to another.
All this has become a possibility thanks to the adoption of mobile payment technologies by the payments industry. Typical payment technologies include mobile payment apps and mobile wallets.

How Do Payment Solutions Work?

The Use of online payment methods has helped simplify transactions and enabled the world to become a global village. Unlike cash transactions, there are usually tons of activities occurring behind the scenes when using digital payments methods.

Once a buyer has selected their preferred payment solution, the bank and payment processors immediately initiate a series of actions and notifications. All these will result in a debit or credit of the amounts transacted to the accounts belonging to the seller and buyer.
