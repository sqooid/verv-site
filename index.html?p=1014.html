Fintech uses new, digital and online technologies in the banking and financial industry, covering various cashless payments and crowdfunding platforms. Verv is one of the most upcoming fintech companies offering business-oriented services. Verv does not only concentrate on providing financial assistance but also provides non-bank services.

Financial technology developments are assisting small businesses in upgrading themselves. It provides several options relating to payment processing, funding, and money transfer. According to the CNBC business news report, fintech achieved $39 billion in 2018, a 100% increase from the previous year.

Which Players are Involved in Fintech?

 Fintech Companies and Non-Banks

Fintech is a company that has a direct interaction with consumers. The primary role of these companies is to provide financial services and connect products to financial services.

Banking as a Service

Banking as a service platform provides the software that ensures safe data transmission between the fintech company and the bank. These companies act as a middle layer between the two. This company provides digital payments and processing links to the services.

The New Age or Physical Bank

The bank is responsible for offering infrastructure as a service. It contains the right hardware equipment that performs server and communication platforms. Banks have licenses that allow them to provide financial services. This license will enable them to open their banking systems to third parties.
