  1. Introducing Address Verification Services (AVS)

Before you initiate any transaction on a payment processing platform, you have to enter your address together with your preferred digital payments method. This is where the AVS comes in. It is an important security measure designed to detect fraud during online transactions.

When any customer makes a purchase, they will have to include their ZIP code and billing address. The AVS system will come into play to verify whether the keyed in information matches.
