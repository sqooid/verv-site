Business Account – White Label Solution

The White Label Solution from Verv is ready, easy to set up, customizable to meet your business needs, and doesn’t have any red tape. Its top features include:

  • Payment Platform: Take advantage of the Verv software, network, certifications, permissions, and licenses to kickstart a branded platform of your own. All the hard work has already been done for you.
  • Prepaid Cards: Use physical and virtual prepaid cards created under your brand identity. Determine your preferred markup fees and benefit from new customer subscriptions and every transaction completed using these cards.

Technology Used by Verv to Support Its Business Solutions

Verv guarantees its clients unparalleled security that relies on multi-decentralized private blockchain networks to guarantee data security. The technology, which has been described as fortified and un-hackable, ensures that data doesn’t get interfered with during the transfer process.

The technology shreds encrypt and distribute the information across numerous immutable distributed ledgers. The technology distributes the data across different chains in the form of puzzle pieces.
It then rebuilds the data once secure authentication using a multi-signature logic has been completed.

What Makes this Technology from Verv So Unique?

The system used by Verv has complied fully with data privacy and regulatory security standards of the US and the EU. Some of these standards include PCI-DSS, SCA, and GDPR. The features that make this technology so unique include:

  • Impossible to hack or attack with a malware
  • Customers will continue to own their private information at all times
  • Information never leaves the customer’s private storage area
  • The client can give access to third parties if and when they want
  • At any one time, only one client having one device and one key can access the stored data.
